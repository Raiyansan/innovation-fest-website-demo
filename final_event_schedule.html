Final Round Guidelines and Schedule of MIST INTER-UNIVERSITY ICT INNOVATION FEST - 2021

  • Heartiest congratulations to all the teams who have made it to the Final Round of the Hackathon, Project Showcasing, and Mobile App Contest events of the MIST INTER-UNIVERSITY ICT INNOVATION FEST - 2021.

    As you know, the final round for these events where winners in each category of each event will be selected will be held on 28-29 Oct 2021 (Thursday and Friday).

    While we would have loved to host all of you at MIST and hold the event in a festive manner, due to the current situation, we have decided to conduct the event fully online.

    As such, each team has been given a specific slot when they’ll need to present their idea/project/app in front of the judges’ panel. Please take a look at your respective event’s guidelines and schedule below.

    The guidelines include general instructions and the judgment criteria for respective events. For smooth conduct of the events, please follow them closely.

    All the best for the final round! We hope to see you soon.
