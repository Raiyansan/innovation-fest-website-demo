Project Showcasing

  • Registration Deadline: 12th October, 2021
  • Eligible Candidates: University Students of BD
  • Registration Fee: 100 BDT per student
  • Team Formation: 3 to 5 members
  • Overview
  • The world is moving fast and innovation in the field of information and technology has made it even quicker. It is very important to embrace different skill-set and abilities to ensure adaptability and transformation that will increase efficiency in a sustainable way. Thus, Dept of CSE, MIST brings you the opportunity to showcase your latent talents by showcasing some of your creative projects. This could be any impactful and innovative project that you and your team have developed during the pandemic in the field of information technology, science and engineering. The project can be software based, hardware based or a mixture of both the modalities.
    Five prizes will be given for the Project showcasing, which are as follows.
    First prize: 20,000 BDT
    Second prize: 18,000 BDT
    Third prize: 15,000 BDT
    Fourth prize: 12,000 BDT
    Fifth prize: 10,000 BDT

Expected Projects

  • A fully implemented project with workable features that identifies a problem and provides the solution through the project.
    Topic areas include, but are not limited to:
  • Topics:
  • Software Engineering & Programming
  • Graphics & Computer-Aided Design
  • Databases & Information Systems
  • Networks and Communications
  • Image Processing & Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence
  • Hardware, Robotics & Electronics

Submission Guidelines

  • Project Synopsis: Not more than 400 words. Synopsis should include Title of the project, scope, objectives/ main motivations, features, methodology, about the Hardware and/or Software used, implementation, limitations (if any) and conclusion.
  • Video File: A video file explaining all the features of the project. Implementation, workable features should be the main focus of the video. Video length will be 2 - 3 mins. Upload your video to www.youtube.com as an unlisted video and share the video URL along with the project synopsis [Optional].
  • N.B. If the project is solely based on mobile application, it is encouraged to submit the project in the Mobile App segment of the event.

Judgment Criteria

  • Relevance: Clear and focused purpose. Identifies contribution to the field of study. Outcome of the project is relevant to the theme of the competition.
  • Creative Ability: project demonstrates significant creativity in one or more criterias
  • Design and Methodology: Well designed plan and implementation methods Execution: Full features executed properly.

Timeline

  1. Problem Statement disclosure: September 5, 2021
  2. Round 1 Submission: October 12, 2021
  3. Declaration of Shortlisted Teams: October 19, 2021
  4. Final Round Presentation: October 28-29, 2021
  5. Declaration of Winners: October 30, 2021*
  • * N.B. The exact mode for the final round and the closing ceremony will be circulated soon.
